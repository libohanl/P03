ABOUT ME

I'm a forth year SIAT Student and currently focusing on studying film making abilities such as photographing, editing and colour correction. To me , my major provides a platform and opportunity to reinforce my skills, also makes us able to experience entire film production process and acquire knowledges from it.

Haida Now

Local Haida Artists shared their insights and knowledge about the art pieces, providing visitors with the opportunity to experience a powerful way to engage with the worldview and sensibility of the Haida people while gaining greater appreciation for the role museums can play in the reconciliation movement. During the process of creating this documentary, our team had group meeting once a week. Mostly, we assigned weekly jobs to each group member and each of us would bring the outcomes on next week's meeting.   For the filming stage, my role was to set up an A-roll camera and record some B-rolls as well. After that, I did transcribing on 10 mins of our A-rolls and typed them on google drive. On editing stage, one of my teammates was the film editor and we gave assistances to him.In the end, I used adobe premiere to colour corrected the entire film ,and now the film is ready to play.

Oops

At the start of this project, when we were still deciding on what storyline to go for, we were thinking of having the protagonist (a domino piece) beating up on multiple other toys which involves a bunch of character animation that we could not accomplish within the short period of time. Therefore, after talking within the group, we decided rather to go for an action short film, we want to do something that has a feeling of pixar animation. Our short was inspired by Garfield, a scene where Garfield “accidentally” messed up the room. Our group aimed for a comedic feeling towards the film while also having detailed character expression and movement supported with realistic environment animation.
